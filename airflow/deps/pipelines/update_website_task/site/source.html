Hi! This website was created to identify interesting flat sale & rent opportunities in Poland. It presents only offers from last week, as all of them get quickly outdated. Results are updated daily, around 1am Central European Time.
Here's a really minimal summary of how things work:

  1. Valuation model is trained based on signification amout of historical data from the polish real-estate market (>200K sale and >150K rent offers)
  2. Each new offer is assigned an estimate value.
  3. Offers from the last week are sorted based on difference between square meter prediction and offer price. The ones with biggest differece are presented here.

If you'd like to find out more about technical internals of this project (tools used, pipeline architecure, ML behind the predictions, ...) make sure to take a look at projects gihub repo.
